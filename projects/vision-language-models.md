# Vision-Language Models — Experiments & Serving

**Role:** VLM research & serving · **Output:** experiments, demos, served endpoints

## What
Hands-on work with vision-language models for multimodal understanding and demos:

- **Models:** LLaVA-NeXT (vendored & extended), Qwen-VL, Gemma multimodal experiments.
- **Serving:** Qwen served via **Open WebUI** on a Slurm **H100** allocation, giving an
  interactive multimodal chat surface for evaluation and stakeholder demos.
- **Experiments:** image/document understanding pipelines and generation experiments
  (incl. video-generation tooling exploration).

## Focus
- Getting modern VLMs running and *usable* on shared HPC (allocation, offline caches, web UI).
- Evaluating multimodal grounding — connecting to the broader [grounding theme](grounded-qa-contact-center.md)
  of the research work (perception as another grounding source beyond text).

## Stack
LLaVA-NeXT · Qwen-VL · Gemma · Open WebUI · SLURM H100 · Hugging Face.
